Frequently Asked Questions

Are flights included?

Unless specifically stated in the itinerary, international and domestic flights are not included in the tour price.

What is the cancellation policy?

Full refund up to 60 days before departure. 50% refund between 30-60 days. No refund within 30 days.

How fit do I need to be?

Each tour has a physical rating from 1 (Relaxed) to 5 (Challenging). Please check the specific requirements on the trip page.
